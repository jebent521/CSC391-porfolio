FUS Altar Server's Guild

In this team project, we created a webpage for the Altar Server's Guild at Franciscan University. The colors of the webpage were selected to model Franciscan's colors. The fonts were chosen to give the page a Medieval feel. We created a form for students to apply to become altar servers. The form was styled to look like a sheet of paper. On the form, there is a series of checkboxes to allow students to select their availability to serve on-campus Masses. We styled these checkboxes as a row that wraps to the next line when there is not enough space on the page.
